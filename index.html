Hi... If you are reading this, it means the timer has finally run out, and the music is playing. There is so much I have carried in my heart, and today, I just want to lay it down honestly. Thank you for being the patient teacher who sat by me, helped me pass my semester exams, and sheltered me when I needed to sneak my way through them. You were my anchor. You are, without a doubt, the prettiest girl I have ever laid eyes on, both inside and out.

To be completely honest, looking back at everything, I still find myself in absolute disbelief. I look at who you are, and I can't quite wrap my head around the fact that I actually had a girlfriend this beautiful, this eligible, this incredibly talented, and so deeply mature. You were a league above everything else. And that is exactly where the sharpest ache comes from—realizing that letting you slip away is, and will always be, the single biggest regret and the greatest loss of my entire life.

Looking at you now, I see you stepping into a beautiful new chapter. I know this birthday is incredibly special for you—embracing a vibrant new city like Trivandrum, making new friends at CET, discovering more freedom, and welcoming endless surprises. I can picture it all for you: the new best friends, the electric nightlife, the midnight laughter over surprise cake cuttings, the quiet ocean breeze on beach dates, and maybe even new hands reaching out to hold yours. I genuinely pray you enjoy every single second of this beautiful life you are building. You deserve all the magic the world has to offer... but in the middle of all the noise, I hope a small part of you still remembers me.

More than anything, I want to watch you conquer the world from afar. You have always been a force to be reckoned with, and my biggest wish is to see you deeply successful in every single thing you touch—whether you choose to conquer the world as a brilliant techie, an inspiring teacher, or a visionary entrepreneur. I know you will build an incredible career and, someday, a beautiful family of your own, because you are built for greatness.

If we were still together today, I promise you I would have been a better person for you.

I am not rich enough right now to buy you the things you deserve—an iPhone 17 Pro Max, a new scooter, or a Dyson hair care kit. But I wanted to give you something that money can't buy. As an aspiring software developer, I poured my time, my memories of us, and my respect into building this website for you. It is a digital tribute to a girl who is a flawless, priceless gem.

Please know deeply in your heart that this is not a hidden request, a manipulation, or a desperate plea to get back into a relationship. It is simply a clean, genuine gift from a place of pure love and respect to honor a worth gem who changed my life. Happy 22nd Birthday.

Sincerely, — Joe
